Quick Notes

This app uses the 78 Tarot cards with images from the Rider-Waite deck. To enable reversals or choose a different spread, click on the button in the bottom right corner.

The three-card spread represents the past, present, and future. The five-card spread represents the past, recent past, present, near future, and future.

Both upright and reversed meanings will appear in the same popup box. To read a reversed card meaning, scroll down to it.
